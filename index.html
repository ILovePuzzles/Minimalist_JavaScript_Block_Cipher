SUBSTITUTION AND TRANSPOSITION AUTOKEY BLOCK CIPHER





CIPHER INSTRUCTIONS

The library must be made from unique characters, each character cannot appear more than once;
The library must contain at least 2 characters, up to a maximum of 256 characters;
The input message and the two keys can only contain characters from the library;
The message cannot be empty, yet the keys and salt can be generated automatically;
The seeds cannot be more than 128 UTF-16 characters long, and the salt cannot be more than 32 UTF-16 characters long.
Otherwise the seeds and the salt will be truncated;
If your message is not a multiple of the length of the library, then an additionnal character
will be added at the end to complete the message.
